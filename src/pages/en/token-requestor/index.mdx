# What is Token Requestor?

Token Requestor is an **API solution** that provides the capability to securely manage
the tokenization of payment cards from various brands (Visa, Mastercard).

The system enables the conversion of payment card data into **secure tokens**,
reducing the risk of exposing sensitive information and improving the user experience by
eliminating the need to store or transmit sensitive card data.

The platform is designed to integrate securely and efficiently with **merchants, payment providers, and card networks**.


# Main Objective

Reduce the risks associated with handling sensitive information through **card tokenization**,
thereby ensuring a **safer and more reliable payment experience** for both merchants and consumers.
